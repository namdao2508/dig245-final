Investment Management Game

The following game is an investment management game. Its purpose is to teach and educate you how to manage a portfolio and see how your money can work for you.

You, as the player will start out with $100,000. With this money, you are able to invest in three main types of investments: Stocks, Exchange Traded Funds, and Bonds. To see what each of the companies are, you can hover over the names of investments to see their specific descriptions. At each period, you have unlimited time to make changes and trades in your portfolio. In addition, there are news factors you should consider as these are crucial in your portfolio holdings. Once you are ready, you can click the "Next Period" button at the top right. You have 7 periods to grow your portfolio.
